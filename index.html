FAQ

Answers to common questions before project kickoff.

What timeline should I expect?

Usually 10-21 days for a landing page and 3-6 weeks for a multi-page project, depending on scope.

Can we work in stages?

Yes. Discovery, design, build, and launch can be split into separate milestones with clear acceptance.

How do you handle mobile adaptation?

Mobile is designed as a first-class scenario with readable hierarchy, touch-friendly controls, and fast flow.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. There is a post-launch support format for fixes and incremental updates in the first weeks.

Can my team continue the project later?

Yes. Structure and naming are prepared so the project can grow without a full rewrite.

Do you use motion without visual overload?

Yes. Motion is used purposefully to support hierarchy and user flow, not distract from content.
